  • Midtempo Bass
  • // Mediados 2010´s

Midtempo Bass (which goes by Space Bass and a variety of other names) takes the dance-oriented sound of Electronic Dance Music subgenres like Electro House and Brostep, and mixes them with Post-Industrial at mid-tempos around 100 BPM range. Tracks in this style often have a dark atmosphere, repetitive, and bass heavy construction. The genre became known around 2015 and 2016, led by 20 year old female Canadian prodigy REZZ. She grabbed the attention of both Skrillex and deadmau5 where she has done work on both their labels. She has quickly become the leader of the Midtempo Bass movement, using the Space Bass genre name to refer to her sound.
